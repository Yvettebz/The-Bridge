About

The Bridge

Built for the middle space between secondary, postsecondary, and industry.

The Bridge was developed to support counselors and advisors across Utah, Summit, and Wasatch counties. This hub is maintained by the UVU Workforce Alignment Regional Pathways position, created in 2020 through Utah legislation.
